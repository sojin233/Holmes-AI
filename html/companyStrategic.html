Strategic Advisory Board

The Strategic Advisory Board provides advice for strategic decision-making to the HolmesAI Flagship, with long-term impact across the whole initiative. The Strategic Advisory Board also designed KPI to monitor the Flagship's progress towards its main goals, both is research ans innovation and in technical areas, such business strategic and outreach.
